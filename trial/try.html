Inline Element

They are positioned on one line, one after another, until there is enough space on it, and when the line ends, they jump to the next line and fill it in.

    They
  • have display: inline
  • stay on the line as long as there is enough space on it
  • can only be set horizontal geometry: left and right paddings, margins and borders.
  • ignore the top and bottom margin, padding and border values
  • the width and height properties have no effect
